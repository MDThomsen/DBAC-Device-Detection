Product Features
Control lights, electronics, and small appliances.
Requires a SmartThings Hub or a compatible device with SmartThings Hub functionality.
Benefits with SmartThings
Control your Power Outlet and other connected devices with the SmartThings app for iPhone or Android.
Automate your Power Outlet and set it to turn on or off when doors are opened, when there’s activity in your home, and much more.
Manage your Power Outlet and other connected devices with SmartThings Routines for Good Morning, Goodbye, Good Night, and more.
Control your Power Outlet with voice commands using SmartThings and Amazon Alexa.
Notes
Device is also a wireless repeater.
